Lessons Info

Private Instruction offered in Piano, Voice, Ukulele, and Guitar.
Lessons are 45 or 60 minutes in length.

WHAT IS INCLUDED IN EACH LESSON?
Each student is enriched with a well-rounded musical education that includes ear-training, sight-reading, improvisation, composition, memorization tips, technique and music theory, accompaniment styles, and mastering pieces for musical sharing opportunities.

No two students learn the same way, therefore each lesson is personally tailored, catering to each student's individual leaning needs, goals, and strengths.

Learning Materials

A wide variety of material is used depending on the individual. Hannon and Czerny help build a solid foundation of healthy technique. Scales and a hands-on, fun approach to music theory are necessary to equip each student with improvisation skills as well as tools for composition and understanding the pieces they are studying.

Method books are used for the very young beginner (age3) and until level 2-4 depending on the student. Along with method books are a healthy balance of Classical repertoire, fun contemporary pieces, popular songs, Duets, Jazz, Blues, and more!

Baby/Toddler Group Classes
